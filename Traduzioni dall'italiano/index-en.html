How to get to the house

The video above shows how to reach the house by car from the entrance of the town of Stintino to the address provided. The private parking is called "N2".

From the parking lot to the front door

The video above shows how to reach the house from the private parking (N2) to the entrance of the house.


Outside the house there will be a safe that contains the keys. The code is 0202.

From the house to the electricity meter

The light meter is located in this driveway inside a door that is on the left.
When you arrive at the house it will already be active, but in case of a power failure you will have to activate it. The electricity meter of the house is the one with the words "Sergio Carrara" on it.

Turn on air conditioners

Before using the air conditioners, make sure that the switch behind the sofa is on. Once turned on, operate the air conditioners from the appropriate remote controls.

Differentiated garbage collection

You will absolutely have to carry out separate collection. In the house you will find the collection methods and the calendar.
Only after dinner time will you have to leave the bins with the correct garbage in the private parking and in the morning the ecological operators will come by to collect the garbage.
When the operators have finished picking up the garbage, you can take the basket home.
At check out you will have to take out all the garbage (which will not be picked up by the ecological operators) and you will have to go to the local ecological center whose location we provide you with.

Ecological island for departing tourists

In the months of July and August in the locality of Le Saline it will be set up an island manned only for outgoing utilities. The times of opening will be from 8:00 to 10:00 and from 16:00 to 18:00 every day of the week.
